Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Satva

How long does it take to get started?

+

We can typically schedule your free consultation within 2-3 business days. After our initial conversation, we'll provide a detailed proposal within one week. Most clients begin their engagement within 2-4 weeks of first contact.

What information should I prepare for the consultation?

+

Helpful to have: your company's annual revenue, number of employees, EU operations/customers details, and any existing sustainability reporting. However, don't let lack of information prevent you from reaching out—we can help you gather what's needed.

Do you work with companies outside the US?

+

Yes! While we're based in the US and specialize in helping US mid-market companies, we work with clients globally. Our expertise in CSRD compliance is valuable regardless of your headquarters location.

What's included in the free consultation?

+

Your 30-minute consultation includes: CSRD applicability assessment for your company, timeline discussion, high-level budget guidance, identification of key challenges, and next steps recommendation. No sales pressure—just honest guidance.

How do your fees compare to Big Four firms?

+

Our fees typically range from $40K-$150K for comprehensive CSRD compliance support, compared to $500K-$1.5M+ at Big Four firms. We provide the same level of expertise at mid-market pricing, making compliance accessible without sacrificing quality.
