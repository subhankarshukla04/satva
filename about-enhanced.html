About Satva

Founded on Dharmic principles, built for mid-market success

Our Story

Satva Sustainability Advisory & Consulting Services was founded with a clear mission: to make world-class CSRD compliance accessible to mid-market companies who deserve the same level of expertise as large enterprises, but at a price point that makes business sense.

We recognized a critical gap in the sustainability consulting market. While large enterprises have access to Big Four accounting firms and multinational consultancies, mid-market companies—the backbone of the American economy—were left with an impossible choice: pay $500,000+ for expert guidance, or struggle through CSRD compliance alone.

Our founder, Niki Shah, saw this challenge firsthand while working with manufacturing, healthcare, and financial services companies throughout the Northeast. These companies had European customers and operations, triggering CSRD requirements, but lacked the budgets for traditional consulting engagements.

Satva was born from the belief that sustainability compliance should be achievable, affordable, and aligned with core values of integrity and service. We combine deep CSRD expertise with a practical, hands-on implementation approach—building systems, training teams, and creating lasting capabilities within your organization.

Our Dharmic Principles

Six timeless values guide our work, ensuring integrity, compassion, and excellence in every engagement

Satya

Truth / Transparency

We provide honest assessments and transparent pricing. No overselling, no hidden costs. If you don't need a service, we'll tell you. Our reputation is built on trust, not transactions.

Daya

Compassion

We understand that CSRD compliance can feel overwhelming. Our team meets you where you are with empathy, patience, and supportive guidance throughout your sustainability journey.

Dana

Giving

We commit 5% of our revenue to pro bono sustainability work for nonprofits and community organizations. Success means lifting others, not just ourselves.

Ahimsa

Non-Harm

Our work helps companies minimize their negative impacts on people and planet. We approach every engagement asking: "How can we reduce harm and create positive change?"

Seva

Selfless Service

We view our work as service, not just business. Your success is our success. We measure our impact not just in revenue, but in the positive change we help create.

Asteya

Integrity

We respect intellectual property, honor confidentiality, and maintain the highest ethical standards. Your data is your data. Your success is your success.

Why We Focus on Mid-Market Companies

Mid-market companies—those with $10M to $500M in annual revenue—are the engine of the American economy. They create jobs, drive innovation, and serve critical roles in supply chains. Yet when it comes to sustainability consulting, they've been underserved.

The Gap in the Market

Big Four accounting firms and multinational consultancies focus on Fortune 500 clients, charging $500,000 to $1.5 million for CSRD compliance—pricing that's impossible for most mid-market budgets. Solo practitioners and small consultancies, while affordable, often lack the depth of expertise and implementation capabilities needed for complex CSRD requirements.

Our Purpose-Built Approach

We designed Satva specifically for mid-market companies. Our pricing ($40K-$150K per engagement) is accessible without sacrificing quality. Our implementation-focused methodology builds lasting capabilities within your organization, so you're not dependent on consultants forever. And our deep CSRD expertise ensures you get enterprise-grade guidance at mid-market prices.
